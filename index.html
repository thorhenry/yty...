Welcome To YTY, The destination of high-quality, sustainable fashion.

YTY is abbreviated from 'Yesu Tela Yoo' which is a mixture of Kiswahili and Acholi language. 'Yesu Tela Yoo' tranlates in English as 'Jesus Lead My Way'. Founded in 2022, we are a brand that values style, comfort, and ethical production practices. we believe that fashion can be both beatiful and responsible, and we are committed to creating clothes that look good and feel good too. Our product range includes a variety of pieces, from everyday essentials to statement pieces, all made with premium materials and expert craftsmanship. Join us on our journey to more sustainable future with fashion that you can feel good about wearing.
